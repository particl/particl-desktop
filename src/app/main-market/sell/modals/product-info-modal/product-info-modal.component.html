Product Templates

The product template serves as a default source to describe a single product in your inventory.

A single product may be published on one or more markets, in which case, a template for each of the specific markets can be created. This allows for the product to be customized for the specific market it is published to, while still retaining the default product information.

This allows, for example, a product template to be set up that contains the default product information, such as title and descriptions, images, and shipping information. When cloning this template to a market template to be used to publish the item to a specific market, the market template begins with a copy of this default information which can then be adjusted and customized for the specific market that its going to be published to; for example: adjusting the price for the specific market, adding or removing additional images, or customizing further the description of the item.

Note that changes to the product template do not apply to existing market templates. To apply the change to a market template, please edit the market template directly.
