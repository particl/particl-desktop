Create a new Market Identity

Each wallet behaves like a separate Identity – this will basically create a new Market-enabled wallet for you.

The Identity name is simply a means for easy distinction between different Identities (wallets), and is not transmitted in any way.

A market may only be associated with a single identity – it will not be possible to add a market to this identity that has been added to another identity. The identity may also need funds assigned to it in order to transact on the marketplace.
