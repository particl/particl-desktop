Disable Cold Staking

Disable Cold Staking and move your funds back to your wallet from your staking device. Note that you don't need to disable Cold Staking to spend your coins.

Staked coins won't be affected. If the outputs you want to move are in your staked balance, you must wait for them to mature.

Disabling Cold Staking will create {{ utxos.txs.length }} transaction(s) and will consume {{ fee }} PART in fees.
